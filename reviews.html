Reviews from My Past Tour Group Members

Rachel was my tour guide at Hohenschönhausen, the former Stasi prison, in May 2017. A young American historian, native of California,
she was quick to point up her East European and Jewish ancestry, and several years residency in Berlin. All of which gave her a uniquely
distanced, yet very grounded and very knowledgeable perspective on the history of Hohenschönhausen. She took every opportunity to
engage with the group and answered all our questions with enthusiasm and intelligence. However, what I most appreciated about Rachel
was her levity throughout. Hohenschönhausen is somewhere that has been witness to the most unspeakable crimes. But the manner in
which Rachel conducted the tour ensured that it was never too emotionally wrought, and not at all the gruelling 2 hours it could have been.
In sum total, Rachel's a great tour guide. And a warm, passionate, smart individual.
— Matthew Robinson, London

Rachel led a fascinating tour of the Berlin Wall Memorial (Bernauerstrasse) for my high school group last summer and she was simply
amazing. She is a proverbial wealth of knowledge and her passion and excitement for the material she presented was contagious. She
has an unusual ability to enrapture her audiences with her tales of the past, bringing the time and events alive in a way that appeals to all
ages. I highly recommend her as a tour guide and look forward to working with her again!
— Dr. Susannah Martin, Senior Program Leader for Berlin, Council on International Educational Exchange

Rachel is an experienced, knowledgeable, and personable guide. She guided my group of 20 university students at both
Sachsenhausen Concentration Camp and again at the Hohenschönhausen Prison Camp in Berlin. She has a unique ability to
contextualize and personalize Germany’s difficulty history. I would highly recommend her tours to anyone who wants a serious,
informative, and very interesting introduction to Germany’s past.
— Dr. Charles Vannette, Assistant Professor of German, Berlin Program Director

Unsere Gruppe war sehr begeistert von Rachels Führung an der Gedenkstätte Berliner Mauer. Sie hat sehr
anschaulich und lebhaft erzählt und informiert. Man konnte ihr gut Folgen und wurde zum Nachdenken angeregt.
— Ulrike Krone, Deutsch-Amerikanisches Institut, Tübingen
